Free · Open Source
Google Fonts
Enormous library. Filter aggressively — the gems are buried. Best used for body/mono pairings rather than display. Avoid the top 20 most popular entirely.
fonts.google.com
free
Fontshare
Curated by Indian Type Foundry. High quality, all free, dramatically less saturated than Google. Several genuinely strange and beautiful options. Start here.
fontshare.com
free · priority
Velvetyne
French experimental collective. Nothing here is safe or predictable. Genuinely weird, genuinely free. The correct register for this project. Look at everything.
velvetyne.fr
free · priority
Use & Modify
Open source fonts only, well curated and organized. Good for finding things you wouldn't encounter on Google. Strong on geometric and editorial cuts.
usemodify.com
free · open source
Adobe Fonts
Requires Creative Cloud. Extensive library including many foundry exclusives. Worth mining if you already have a subscription. Quality is consistently high.
fonts.adobe.com
free w/ cc
Paid · Worth It
Typography.com — H&FJ
Hoefler & Co. The serious industry standard. Expensive, meticulous, timeless. Gotham, Sentinel, Chronicle. Not experimental but irreproachably excellent.
typography.com
paid · premium
Commercial Type
Editorial. Distinctive. Used by major publications worldwide. Foundry for people who take type seriously. Strong historical sensibility fused with contemporary cuts.
commercialtype.com
paid · editorial
Klim Type Foundry
New Zealand. Exceptional and genuinely unusual. Tiempos, Domaine, Founders Grotesk. Not trying to be trendy. The kind of type that ages into mythology. High priority for this project.
klim.co.nz
paid · priority
Dinamo — ABC Dinamo
The exact register this project needs. Used by Björk collaborators, experimental publications, and institutions that take aesthetics seriously. Feels like it belongs to a different century without being costume-y. Start here for the xenographic archival feel.
abcdinamo.com
paid · top priority
Swiss Typefaces
Swiss precision with genuinely unexpected options. Suisse Int'l and its variants are widely used but the catalog has quieter gems. Clean, exacting, serious.
swisstypefaces.com
paid · swiss
Future Fonts
Fonts in active development — cheaper because they're unfinished, genuinely experimental because the makers are still figuring them out. Best place to find something nobody else has yet. The discovery layer.
futurefonts.xyz
paid · experimental
For this project specifically: the xenographic archival register wants something that reads as ancient and alien simultaneously. Look for serifs with unusual stroke contrast, monospaced cuts with personality, or grotesques that feel slightly wrong in a way you can't immediately name. Dinamo and Velvetyne first. Klim and Future Fonts second. Avoid anything in the top results of any of these sites — the right font will be three pages in.
